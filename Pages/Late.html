Almost There

When will it end?

When going into the late game you will notice a huge leap from everything before. Your damage will sky rocket, Your stats will be amazing and the money will flow in.
But you need to know what to upgrade so choose:

  • Mining

  • Foraging

  • Farming

  • Combat

  • Money

Mining

If you did mining as intended you should have reached 4M mithril powder by now. The powder will help you gain massive stat boosts that you will use until the end.

Foraging

As said in the middle guide foraging doesn't change that much but it does have a slight change.
At level 20 you unlock the "Treecapitator" which works the same way as a jungle axe but better, breaking 31 logs. In addition after you killed some dragongs you should have the "Young Dragon set" which is the fastest set in the game.

Young Dragon Set The Treecapitator

Farming

Late game farming actually becomes quite nice because of the new island they added to the game called the garden. To enter this island talk with Sam.
In this late game island you have presets premade for you for each crop in the game which saves alot of time building.
Each crop now has a unique harvesting tool that doubles or TRIPLES the crops given by each break. that can be bought from Anita by completing Jacob's quests.


The Garden Sugercane Hoe Jacob Sam and Anita

You also need way smaller farms because the crops grow faster and in addition give more farming xp.





Combat

Combat in the late game starts to be really interesting because you have this boss called Kuudra that gives you the ability to go with whatever style of combat you like. (Combat level 24 needed).
The 4 styles are:
Berserker, Crimson. You have high defence and use swords to attack with a slashing attack once in a while
Archer, Terror. You are more speedy and all your bows will gain 2+ arrows making you shoot 3 at a time.
Mage, Aurora. You find yourself using all sort of weapons from beam shooting and exploding tp ones to fire casting wands but you will be very squishy so careful.
Summoner, Fevor. You will have the ability to summon enemys you killed as your minions to fight for you.

Money

In this stage of the game everything you do will be profitable but some are more then others.
For the combat route fight kuudra in the Crimson isles, the reward chest give alot of money.
For the farming route harvest netherwarts (found in the garden) with the right hoe for most the most amount of money.
For the mining route use the event that happens in the Dwarven Mines where all drops are doubled for 15 minutes and mine mithril.
