The End

Is it over?

When going into the end game you feel like you done it, now everything that you want to do is possible but not without the right things. The main problem you encounter in the end game is money so just be sure that you have enough.
Choose what you want to be the best at:

  • Mining

  • Foraging

  • Farming

  • Combat

  • Money

Mining

Here you will find the final upgrade for your mining setup, The Divan armor and Gemstone Gaunlet.
There is a new part of the Dwarven mines that opened for you and you can get there by paying 10K coins to Gwendolyn. There you will find gemstones that worth way more then mithril.

Divan Armor set The Gemstone Gaunlet Gwendolin


The place you get to is called the Crystal Hollows where you have new type of minerals to mine named gemstone. gemstone are the end game mineral that is the hardest to mine and gives the most money.
To mine as efficent as possible find yourself or search for a route where gemstones batches always spawn and grind that to level up fast.

Crystal hollows center Gemstone veins

Foraging

Foraging doesn't have any change but if you feel like you don't want to do it there is a way around doing foraging.
A speicel mayor, Jerry, that is elected once every 5 months drops loot boxes every 6 minutes that give you foraging xp, then if you have 5B coins, buy them in the bazaar.

Mayor Jerry Jerry box

Farming

Othere then maxing your hoe by farming alot you have one last upgrade that is the Fermento armor set that give a huge boost to farming fortune.
Fermento armor pieces drop randomly at a rate of 1/30,000 every time you break a crop in the garden so I hope you are lucky.


Fermenro set

Money

This is the most important part of the end game so find the strat that gives you the most and is the most enjoyable
For the combat route fight ghosts that you find in the dwarven mines under the main bridge.
For the farming route harvest netherwarts (found in the garden) with the right hoe for most the most amount of money, and sell any fermento armor drops.
For the mining route find the best gemstone route you can find and perfect it to the max.
Foraging still sucks so you can learn how to bazzar/AH flip Here, and mybe try to learn how to lowball.
