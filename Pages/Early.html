Starting Your Adventure

So what do you need?

When you first start you'll have no skills and skill are important threw out the whole game no matter what.
So choose what you want to do first.

  • Mining

  • Foraging

  • Farming

  • Combat

Mining

So you choosed to do mining, great! Mining ups your defense stats and at the end will benefit you for a lot of money.
To start your mining adventure go to the coal mine directly forward when joining the hub. on your way talk to the Mine Merchant and buy a promising pickaxe for 35 coins, it will help you get started.

The Mine Merchant's hut The Mine Merchant

Go to the mine and mine some coal until you are level 1. Once you get to level 1 you can enter the gold mine.

there you'll want to get to level 5 to open the Deep Cavrens. There you start to need some armor so go buy some Miner Armor from the auction house.

Here you should also just get a normal diamond pickaxe with fortune 3, efficiency 5 and smelting touch 1 to speed things up


Foraging

So you choosed to do foraging, great! Foraging ups your strength stat and at the end will benefit you for all the game to deal more damage.
To start your foraging adventure go to forest at the left of the spawning point in the hub. On your way there talk to the Lumber Merchant in the Bazaar Alley and buy a golden axe and enchant it with efficiency 5 right away.

The Baazar Alley The Lumber Merchant

Next you'll want to go to the forest and as you expected just chop down the trees until you can go to "The Park" this will be where you do foarging until the rest of the game and is unlocked at level 3

The Forest

The Park

Farming

So you choosed to do farming, great! Farming ups your health stat and at the end will benefit you for all the game to have more resistance againts mobs.
To start your farming adventure go to the farm house directly right of the spawning point in the hub. Talk to the Farm Merchant in the front and buy a rookie hoe and enchant it with harvesting 5 right away.

The Farm House The Lumber Merchant The Rookie hoe

Next you will want to go to the Farm which is located just ahead and mine some wheat. This is also a good way to make money in the early game as wheat sells for quite alot.

The Farm

Combat

So you choosed to do combat. Combat isn't that great to start with because you don't have the necessary equipment, but do as you wish. Combat ups your damage and crit chance stat and at the end will benefit you for all the game to deal way more damage.
To start your combat adventure go to The Graveyard at the left of the spawning point in the hub. On your way there talk to the Weaponsmith in the Bazaar Alley and buy an Undead Sword and enchant it with smite 5 right away.

The Undead Sword The Weaponsmith

Once you're in The Graveyard you will want to kill zombies untill you are combat level 3 and then you can move on to fight tougher mobs such as spiders in the Spider's Den.

The Graveyard

The Spider's Den
