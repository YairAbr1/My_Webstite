You Need To Get Better

So how to keep going?

When going into the middle game you will have a little bit of skills and enough progress to start harder things but now money comes into play.
So choose what you want to do first?

  • Mining

  • Foraging

  • Farming

  • Combat

  • Money

Mining

To keep going with mining you need to get to mining level 12 to unlock the Dwarven Mines where you can mine mithril and do missions to get more xp and powder. To get there just talk to the Lift Operator
Mining has a perk tree that you can unlock the more you mine and do missions and every powder will benefit you through out all the game so you really just need to mine and mine.

The Dwarven Mines The Lift Operator


Now you will want to get some upgrades.
first you'll want to buy glacite armor from the auction house and a mithril drill that will speed every thing up. Remember to always enchant your things because it gives a huge boost.

Glacite armor Stats The drill

Foraging

Foraging doesn't change alot through out the game but you should really just care about speed to get from one tree to another faster so just equip the fastest set you have.
At level 12 you open the ability to use the jungle axe that gives you the ability to chop down trees faster because it breaks multiple logs at each swing.

The Jungle Axe

For max efficiency you should really just focus on one style of tree and it is the dark oak tree in the Dark Thicket because it has the most logs in each tree

The Dark Thicket



Farming

Farming is annoying because you don't have a consistent place with enough crops to keep going as much as you need. To solve that problem we will build farms at our island.
To build a farm on your island you should really get a builder's wand from the auction house it will make you place 160 blocks every click instead of 1.
The best farm for money is a suger cane farm and the best farm for xp is pumpkin.


Pumpkin Farm Sugercane Farm

To farm the pumkings just use your golden axe for foraging from the start and for sugercane use your rookie hoe





Combat

Now that you got your combat level high enough you will be able to use way better stuff and fight more rewarding mobs.
You unlocked The End where you can fight dragons and tall creatures called "Endermans".
Your upgrades should be Shadow Assassin armor and an Aspect Of The Dragons. They would give you a massive boost to damage and survivability.
For Shadow Assasin armor you will wan't combat level 12 so it's fine but for Aspect Of The Dragons it's a little bit of a grind. You need combat level 16 but with the end it will be easy.

Shadow Assassin armor AOTD

To kill dragons you need to summon them first so go down the end and kill zealots. you have 1 / 420 chance to get a summoning eye.
Dragons Nest Zealot

Money

So you want to know how to get money. This will hunt you for the rest of the game and can sometimes be boring depending what you do.
A method that works all the time is Auction flipping which is essentially buying things from the auction house at a low price and selling them for more.
But in the middle game you should really just pick something that you enjoy to do. If it's farming, combat or mining you should just do it and you will get enough coins to get you through.
For mining you should really just mine mithril and titanium in the dwarven mines to gain mithril powder for the perk tree and alot of money even in the late game.
For combat you should kill zombies in the graveyard to spawn the mini boss that is there.
For farming just use your island's farm and sell the drops to npcs.
